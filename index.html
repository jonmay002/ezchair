The Author

Investing shouldn't keep you up at night. It should fund the things that get you up in the morning.

Douglas B. May is a retired investment advisor based in Western Colorado and the voice behind EZchair Investor. With over 30 years of wealth management experience, he writes to bring clarity and independence to an increasingly complex financial world.

His work emphasizes proactive strategies over traditional "asset storage." Through his daily Market Letter and serial eBook, Investment Heresies, Doug shares objective, experience-based insights to help investors manage risk and invest with intention.
